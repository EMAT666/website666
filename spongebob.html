Spongebob Squarepants

About Spongebob

Spongebob Squarepants is a yellow sea sponge who enjoys being a cook at Krusty Krab. He lives in the Pacific Ocean and often embarks on various adventures with his friends at Bikini Bottom.

Spongebob and his friends.

Spongebob's Characteristics

  1. He is yellow and has big blue eyes.
  2. He has square pants, hence his name.
  3. He is nice, friendly, funny, fun and silly.
